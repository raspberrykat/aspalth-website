ASPHATL 9

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush with Asphalt 9: Legends, the premier mobile racing game that redefines the genre. Featuring over 200 high-performance cars from the world's top manufacturers like Ferrari, Porsche, and Lamborghini, Asphalt 9: Legends offers a stunning roster of vehicles to race, collect, and customize.

Tear through over 80 breathtaking tracks set in dynamic locations around the globe, from the bustling streets of Tokyo to the sun-soaked shores of the Caribbean. Each race is an explosive experience with gravity-defying stunts, precise drifting, and heart-pounding nitro boosts.

Whether you thrive on intense solo challenges or the thrill of multiplayer competition, Asphalt 9: Legends has something for every racing enthusiast. Dive into a rich single-player Career mode or join forces with players worldwide in real-time multiplayer races. Climb the leaderboards, unlock exclusive rewards, and participate in limited-time events that keep the excitement fresh and the stakes high.

Customize your dream car with a variety of colors, rims, and more to stand out on the track. Harness the innovative TouchDrive™ controls for a more immersive racing experience, or take full control of your car with traditional tap-to-steer mechanics.

Join a community of millions and become a racing legend in Asphalt 9: Legends. The race of your life awaits – are you ready to hit the road?
