Celebrate Lunar New Year with Asphatl 9: ReJends' Exclusive Chinatown Track

Posted on February 10, 2024
5 min

Asphatl 9: ReJends is set to usher in the Lunar New Year with a spectacular new addition that promises to delight racers and fans alike. In celebration of this vibrant festival, Gamefolt and RJStudio are proud to introduce the exclusive Chinatown Track, a dazzling new racecourse that brings the sights and sounds of Lunar New Year to life.

Chinatown Track is a feast for the senses, with its intricate decorations, glowing lanterns, and bustling streets capturing the essence of the Lunar New Year festivities. Racers will navigate through narrow alleyways, over ornate bridges, and past stunning pagodas, all while being immersed in a rich tapestry of traditional music and fireworks.

This limited-time track is not just about aesthetics; it offers a thrilling racing experience with tight corners, sudden elevation changes, and strategic shortcuts. Players will need to master their drifting skills and nitro boosts to conquer the course and claim victory.

In addition to the new track, Asphatl 9: ReJends is rolling out exclusive Lunar New Year events and rewards. Racers can look forward to special time-limited challenges, themed decals, and unique vehicle upgrades that celebrate the spirit of the season. Completing these challenges will earn players festive in-game items and bonuses, adding a layer of excitement and competition to the celebrations.

Gamefolt and RJStudio have gone above and beyond to ensure that this Lunar New Year is an unforgettable experience for the Asphatl 9 community. The Chinatown Track is a testament to their commitment to providing fresh and engaging content, while honoring cultural traditions and bringing players together.

Join the festivities and experience the thrill of racing under the lantern-lit skies of the Chinatown Track. This Lunar New Year, Asphatl 9: ReJends invites you to celebrate in high-speed style and create unforgettable memories on the track. Don't miss out on this exclusive event - race your way into the Lunar New Year today!
